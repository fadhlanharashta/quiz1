BANDUNG CULINARY

Bandung is renowned for its rich and diverse culinary scene, offering a wide range of delicious dishes that reflect the city's unique blend of Indonesian, Chinese, and Western influences. Some of the most famous bandung food are surabi, cuanki, and karedok.
