BANDUNG BERMARTABAT

Bandung is the third-largest city in Indonesia and the capital of the West Java province. It is located in the mountainous region of the island of Java, about 140 km southeast of Jakarta, the capital city of Indonesia. Bandung has a population of around 2.5 million people, making it one of the most populous cities in Indonesia.
Bandung has a long and rich history, with evidence of human habitation dating back to the 4th century AD. The city was an important trade center during the colonial era, with the Dutch establishing a military post there in the 17th century. In the early 20th century, Bandung became known for its modern and innovative architecture, earning it the nickname "Paris van Java" or "Paris of Java."
