BANDUNG TOURISM

Bandung is a city in West Java, Indonesia, that is known for its beautiful natural scenery, rich cultural heritage, and historical significance. There are several tourist attractions in Bandung that are worth visiting, including the seat of the governor of West Java Gedung Sate, Gedung Merdeka a historical building used during the Asia Africa Conference, and a mall themed like european cities Paris Van Java Mall (PVJ).
